About Me:

Ke Liang received the Ph.D. degree from National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) advised by Prof. Xinwang Liu. Prior to joining NUDT, he was advised by Prof. Sharon Huang as a Research Support and a PhD student at College of Information Sciences and Technology in Pennsylvania State University (PSU) for 2 years from 2020. Meanwhile, he received Master of Science degree under the supervision of Prof. Gang Tan majored in Computer Science and Engneering at PSU. Before that, he got his bachelor's degree at Beihang University (BUAA). Track records for all have been proven excellent.

Research Interests: Graph Learning, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Multi-view Clustering
