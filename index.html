Short Bio
I am current a Master student majored in Computer Science and Engneering at Pennsylvania State University.
My research interests are Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition, Image Processing, and Machine Learning, focusing on methods for object recognition, image and video segmentation, motion tracking, multimodal tasks and other AI-assisted medical diagnosis tools. I also have the experiences on the research for applying machine learning in program analysis to detect/fix bugs in C programs.
